Elden Ring is an open-world action role-playing game set in a dark fantasy world known as the Lands Between, where players freely explore vast environments filled with dangerous enemies, powerful bosses, hidden dungeons, and mysterious lore. Players take on the role of a Tarnished, an outcast called back to reclaim the shattered Elden Ring, strengthen their character through combat and exploration, and ultimately strive to become the Elden Lord.
