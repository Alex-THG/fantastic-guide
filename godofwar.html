God of War is an action-adventure game that follows the journey of Kratos, a former Greek god, and his son Atreus as they navigate the realms of Norse mythology. The game combines intense combat, puzzle-solving, and exploration, with a strong emphasis on storytelling and character development. Players engage in visceral battles using a variety of weapons and abilities while uncovering the rich lore of the Norse gods and creatures.
